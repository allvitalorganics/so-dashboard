FORMAT — DUAL-CAP

25ml liquid shot + 2 tablets sealed in one unit. Twist open → take tablets → drink shot. Under 30 seconds. No water, no scoop, no blender.

Why dual-cap: Collagen peptides dissolve best in liquid for rapid absorption. Fat-soluble vitamins (E, Beta-Carotene) and minerals are most stable as tablets. One unit = optimal bioavailability + convenience + novel ritual.

Flavor: Pineapple. 28 units/box = 4 weeks (1/day).

Box: Magenta (#B8255A). "skin support by sensitive ones." "Skin Hydration + Glow." "Pineapple Flavored Dual Delivery Formula for Skin Beauty."

Dictionary

So the whole team speaks the same language

PRODUCT TERMS

Verisol®

Specific bioactive collagen peptide by GELITA AG (Germany). Not generic collagen. Multiple published RCTs. SO uses Verisol specifically.

Dual-cap

25ml liquid + 2 tablets in one sealed unit. Twist → take → drink. Under 30 seconds.

Fairy dusting

Adding tiny amounts (e.g. 50mg) just to list on label. Not enough to work. SO's Klaviyo Email 1.2 educates on this.

Proprietary blend

Legal way to list ingredients without individual amounts. Only total shown. SO's core: "No proprietary blends."

S/F claim

Structure/function claim. How a nutrient affects body structure/function. Legal with DSHEA disclaimer + 30-day FDA notification.

NDI

New Dietary Ingredient. Pre-Oct 1994 = no NDI needed. Pine Bark + HA may need verification.

Sodium Hyaluronate

Sodium salt of HA. More stable, better absorbed orally. What SO uses for 120mg dose.

D-Alpha Tocopherol

Natural Vitamin E (vs synthetic dl-alpha). More bioavailable. 30mg = 200% DV.

Pinus pinaster

Maritime pine bark species. Pycnogenol® = branded version. SO uses generic extract.

BUSINESS TERMS

DSHEA

Dietary Supplement Health and Education Act (1994). Defines supplements as separate from drugs.

cGMP

Current Good Manufacturing Practice. 21 CFR Part 111. COSMAX must comply.

FSVP

Foreign Supplier Verification Program. SO LLC must verify COSMAX compliance.

KORUS FTA

US-Korea Free Trade Agreement. 0% duty on Korean supplements.

SAFE

Simple Agreement for Future Equity. Standard pre-seed investment instrument.

COA

Certificate of Analysis. Lab proof of identity, potency, purity. Required by Amazon + cGMP.

CAC

Customer Acquisition Cost. SO target: $35-50 (industry $45-65).

LTV

Lifetime Value. At 6mo avg: $414. At 12mo: $828.

CM1/CM2

CM1 = Gross profit - fulfillment. CM2 = CM1 - marketing. CM2 = real profitability.
